FAQs

  1. When should I visit Baalbek?
    • According to our sources, the best time to visit Baalbek, Lebanon is from December to May to avoid extreme heat.
  2. What about hotels?
    • Worry not, traveller! There are two hotels in Baalbek, one being the Hotel La Memoire and the other being the Hotel Jupiter.
  3. Should I go with a guide?
    • Yes, absolutely. Baalbek, while a lot of fun, is exceptionally confusing for people who have never been.
  4. Is it safe to travel to Baalbek?
    • Yes. While Baalbek is close to the Syrian border, as long as you are careful and respectful, you will be fine.
